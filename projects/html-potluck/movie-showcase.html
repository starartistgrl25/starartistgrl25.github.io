Beauty and the Beast

Genre:Disney/Romance

Runtime:2 hours and 9 minutes.

Rating

Synopsis

 

An arrogant prince is cursed to live as a terrifying beast until he finds true love. Strangely, his chance comes when he captures an unwary clockmaker, whose place is then taken by his bold and beautiful daughter Belle. Helped by the Beast's similarly enchanted servants, including a clock,a teapot and a candelabra, Belle begins to see the sensitive soul behind the fearsome facade. But as time runs out, it soon becomes obvious that Belle's cocky suitor Gaston is the real beast of the piece.

Freakier Friday

Genre:Disney/comedy

Runtime: 1hour and 51 minutes.

Rating

Synopsis

 

Freakier Friday is the sequel to Disney's Freaky Friday, where Tess (Jamie Lee Curtis) and her now-adult daughter Anna (Lindsay Lohan) face a new body swap, this time with Anna's daughter Harper and her future stepdaughter Lily, as they merge their families and prepare for Anna's wedding, leading to another magical mix-up that forces them to confront blended family issues, old flames (Jake!), and new challenges.

Dr. Lily Houghton enlists the aid of wisecracking skipper Frank Wolff to take her down the Amazon in his ramshackle boat. Together, they search for an ancient tree that holds the power to heal -- a discovery that will change the future of medicine.
